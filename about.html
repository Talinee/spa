Who we are?

Siam Spa, an exclusive spa with utmost privacy and premium facility, was established in Chiang Mai since 2002. For almost two decades we have been in the beauty business, Siam Spa has been focusing on using the fineness available products that only derived from naturally ingredients for our clients. The Spa covers an area over 700 sq.m., with spa specialists and professional staffs to attend to the modern women’s needs. Centrally Located just five minutes’ walk from Maya or One Nimman

What to Expect?

We provide number of wonderful spa treatments that you can choose from that will relieve your stress and rejuvenate the body. You may choose to refresh your skin by scrubbing off dead skin cells with a Body Scrub Treatment. Soothe your senses with a Thai Oil Massage or an Aromatherapy Massage. Or try something new to relax your muscles with the Thai Herbal Ball and Thai Massage package. Be prepared to enjoy a day of well-deserved pampering and discover what the best life has to offer. Just bring your desire for great personal care and the anticipation of deep relaxation. It is not necessary to bring special clothing; we will provide a robe for use while you are being pampered.
